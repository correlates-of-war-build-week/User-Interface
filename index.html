Welcome to
War Stories project

Our focus is on determining what regions in the world are likely going to experience war. We take into account factors from the economics to the religion of a region. We will provide you with a percentage of how likely your region will be in war.

Data

We were able to work with correlates of war. They provided us with different datasets that touched upond different datapoints. The data provided was holistic and takes into account many factors. The collection of the data was done in a manner that we trust. You can find more information about the data if you visit this website.

Filter

Our team of three data scientists were able to go through all the data and clean up the data so that we would be able to work with it. This included anything from removing datasets that were not relevant to cleaning up faulty data (which is expected in large datasets).

Result

Once the data sets were cleaned the data scientists were able to run models that took into account all the relevant factors to determine the likelihood of war breaking out in specific regions. Anyone is now able to look at a country and see the percent of how likely it will go to war.

Map

If you register and login with us you will gain access to a map with all the information that was discussed. This includes the percentage for each country along with important information that is relevant to that speciific nation.
